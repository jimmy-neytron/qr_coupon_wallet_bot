Privacy Policy

QR Coupon Wallet is a Telegram Mini App for saving QR coupons and text promo codes.

Data we process

We may process your Telegram user ID, username, first name, saved coupons, QR codes, promo codes, groups, favorites and app preferences.

How we use data

We use this data only to provide the app functionality: saving coupons, organizing groups, syncing data and restoring access.

Data storage

App data is stored in Supabase. We do not sell user data.
